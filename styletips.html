Styling Tips

This page is key to help you find your personal style and perfect it.

Dos and Don'ts

  • Dos
    1. Accessorise with bracelets, dainty necklaces and earrings.
    2. Invest in quality items such as bags, shoes and jackets.
    3. To add a little something to a basic outfit you could roll up the sleeves, tie up the top or add a belt.
  • Don'ts
    1. Don't go overboard when accessorising. For example, if you are wearing a flashy necklace, do not also wear flashy earrings.
    2. Don't wear the same fit of clothing both on the top and bottom. For example, if you wanted to wear a tight fitting top, do not wear tight fitting jeans (e.g. not skinny jeans ). Instead, go for something looser, such as mom jeans. The opposite goes for when you wear a loose fitting top.

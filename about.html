About Us

We strive to help people find their personal style and feel confident.

At Amity Fashion, we strive to help people find their personal style and feel confident. Our name comes from the Latin word 'amity' which means friendship. We chose to use this name to try and make our website more friendly and welcoming to our viewers. Our aim is to make everyone feel themselves and comfortable in what they wear and introduce new styles. At Amity Fashion we also propose new destinations with a fashion history such as Milan which is known as the fashion capital because of its big and well-known fashion week. Furthermore, in these destinations Amity Fashion suggests the best shops for your price range.

We want to make looking for clothes easier and on our site we have included a list of different styles and what they look like. Moreover, Amity Fashion have made it easier to find shops which sell these specific type of clothing.

If you have any further inquiries, you can contact us through our contact forum on the right.
